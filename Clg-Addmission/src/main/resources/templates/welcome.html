Welcome to Admission Process of College


Enrollment in CAP process is compulsory for both the students who have valid score in MH-CET and JEE. As per DTE rule, college can directly admit the candidates on the following seats: 20% of sanction intake seats in each branch (i.e. 12 seats per 60 intake) Vacant seats if any after final CAP round. Contact College authority directly for admission against such seats

Documents required for Admission of Academic Year

  • SSC Mark Sheet
  • HSC Mark Sheet
  • Leaving Certificate
  • MHT-CET Score Card / JEE Score Card
  • Nationality Certificate
  • Domicile Certificate (in students or parents name)
  • Passport Size Photo (2 copy)
  • Aadhar Card (Photo Copy)
  • Bank Account Details
  • Income Certificate
  • Caste Certificate
  • Validity Certificate
  • Non creamy layer Certificate in the name of candidate.
